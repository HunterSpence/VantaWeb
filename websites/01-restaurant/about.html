Meet Our Culinary Team

The passionate artisans who bring our culinary vision to life.

Marcus Blackwood

Executive Chef & Co-Founder

Chef Marcus Blackwood brings over 15 years of culinary excellence to Ember & Oak. Trained at the Culinary Institute of America and refined through experiences in Michelin-starred kitchens across Europe, Marcus has developed a unique approach to flame-grilled cuisine that has earned international recognition.

His passion for fire cooking began during childhood summers at his grandmother's farm, where every meal was prepared over wood fires. This early connection to primal cooking methods would later become the foundation of his culinary philosophy.

Marcus's innovative techniques have been featured in Food & Wine, Bon App√©tit, and James Beard Foundation publications. His commitment to sustainability and local sourcing has made Ember & Oak a leader in responsible fine dining.

Isabella Chen

Sous Chef & Pastry Director

Isabella brings a unique perspective to our culinary team, combining her background in molecular gastronomy with traditional flame techniques. A graduate of Le Cordon Bleu Paris, she spent five years perfecting her craft in some of Tokyo's most innovative restaurants.

Her expertise in pastry and desserts has revolutionized our sweet offerings, creating flame-finished desserts that perfectly complement our savory menu. Isabella's attention to detail and innovative spirit make her an invaluable part of our culinary vision.

When she's not in the kitchen, Isabella can be found experimenting with new flavor combinations or teaching cooking classes to aspiring chefs in our community outreach program.

David Rousseau

Master Sommelier

David is one of only 274 Master Sommeliers worldwide, bringing unparalleled expertise to our wine program. His carefully curated selection focuses on wines that complement the bold, smoky flavors of our flame-grilled cuisine.

With over 20 years in the wine industry and experience at some of the world's finest establishments, David has developed relationships with boutique wineries and exclusive vintners, allowing us to offer rare and exceptional wines you won't find anywhere else.

David's weekly wine dinners and educational tastings have become legendary among wine enthusiasts, creating experiences that deepen appreciation for the perfect marriage of food and wine.

Community & Sustainability

At Ember & Oak, we believe great restaurants are integral parts of their communities. We source over 80% of our ingredients from local farms within a 100-mile radius, supporting sustainable agriculture and reducing our environmental impact.

Our commitment extends beyond our kitchen. We partner with local culinary schools to provide internships, support community food programs, and host educational events that promote culinary arts and sustainable practices.
