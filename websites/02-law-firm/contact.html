Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting our firm

Is the initial consultation really free?

Yes, absolutely. We offer free consultations for most practice areas because we believe everyone deserves access to quality legal advice. During this consultation, we'll evaluate your case, explain your options, and provide an honest assessment of your situation.

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 2 hours during business hours, and within 24 hours on weekends. For urgent matters, we offer 24/7 emergency contact through our emergency line at (555) 911-LAWS.

What should I bring to my consultation?

Bring any documents relevant to your case, such as contracts, correspondence, police reports, medical records, or court papers. If you don't have everything, don't worry - we can help you gather necessary documentation. A list of questions you'd like to ask is also helpful.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer secure video consultations via Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or phone consultations for clients who cannot visit our office. Virtual consultations are just as comprehensive as in-person meetings.

What if my case is outside your practice areas?

If your matter falls outside our expertise, we'll refer you to a trusted colleague who specializes in that area of law. We maintain professional relationships with attorneys across many specialties to ensure you get the best possible representation.
