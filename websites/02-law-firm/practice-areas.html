Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our practice areas and legal services

How do I know which attorney is right for my case?

During your free consultation, we'll evaluate your case and match you with an attorney who has specific experience in your area of need. Each of our attorneys specializes in particular practice areas and has a proven track record in those fields.

What should I bring to my consultation?

Bring any relevant documents related to your case, including contracts, correspondence, police reports, medical records, or court papers. If you don't have everything, don't worry - we can help you gather the necessary documentation.

How long does a typical case take?

Case duration varies significantly depending on the type of legal matter, complexity, and whether it settles or goes to trial. During your consultation, we'll provide an estimated timeline based on the specifics of your situation.

Do you handle cases outside your main practice areas?

While we focus on our core practice areas for optimal results, we maintain relationships with trusted attorneys in other fields. If we can't handle your case directly, we'll refer you to a qualified specialist.

What are your fees and payment options?

Our fee structure varies by practice area. Personal injury cases are typically handled on a contingency basis (no fee unless we win). Other matters may involve hourly rates, flat fees, or retainers. We'll discuss all costs transparently during your consultation.

Don't navigate complex legal matters alone. Our experienced attorneys are ready to fight for your rights and protect your interests.
